🛡️ SugarGuard is a wellness & awareness tool

It is not a medical device and does not diagnose diabetes. Your phone cannot measure real blood glucose by touching your skin.

All sugar figures are estimates based on each food's glycemic index, your portion, timing and the details you enter — not a blood reading.

If you connect a certified CGM or wearable later, real sensor readings are shown separately and clearly labelled.

Always follow your doctor's guidance for any medical decision.
